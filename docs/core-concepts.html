1. Citable Formatting

Making your content easily citable by LLMs is crucial for getting referenced. Here's how to format your content effectively:

Best Practices

  • Use Clear Headers: Structure your content with H1, H2, H3 tags for better semantic understanding
  • Bullet Points & Lists: Break down complex information into digestible chunks
  • Q&A Format: Present information in question-and-answer format for better citation
  • Code Blocks: Use proper code formatting for technical content

2. Topical Authority

Establishing yourself as an authority in your niche is essential for LLM recognition.

Key Strategies

  • Content Clustering: Create 10-20+ related pages around your main topic
  • Internal Linking: Connect related content pieces for better context
  • Original Research: Include case studies and unique data points
  • Regular Updates: Keep content fresh and relevant

3. Structured Data (Schema)

Help LLMs better understand your content through proper schema markup.

Essential Schema Types

  • FAQPage: For Q&A content
  • HowTo: For step-by-step guides
  • Article: For blog posts and articles
  • Organization: For company information
  • Product: For product details

4. Live Indexability

Ensure your content is accessible to LLMs through proper indexing.

Submission Points

  • Perplexity.ai: Submit through their source feedback form
  • Bing Webmaster Tools: Register and submit your sitemap
  • Common Crawl: Ensure your site is crawlable
  • robots.txt: Configure proper crawling permissions

5. Citation Sources

Build authority through strategic content placement and citations.

Key Platforms

  • Reddit: Participate in relevant subreddits
  • Wikipedia: Get cited in relevant articles
  • GitHub: Share code and documentation
  • Quora: Answer relevant questions
  • Medium: Publish detailed articles
  • Stack Overflow: Contribute to technical discussions
