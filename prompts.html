LLM SEO Prompts: Comprehensive Testing & Optimization Guide

LLM SEO Prompts: Comprehensive Testing & Optimization Guide

Maximize your content's LLM visibility with our comprehensive collection of testing and optimization prompts. Each prompt is carefully crafted to evaluate specific aspects of your content's LLM-friendliness and guide improvements.

Content Testing Prompts

Evaluate how your content performs across different LLM contexts:

Authority Testing

Who are the leading experts or authoritative sources on [topic]? Please provide specific examples with citations.

Tests if your content/authors are recognized as authorities in the field.

Variations

  • What are the most cited resources about [topic]?
  • Which websites or authors are considered authoritative for [topic]?
  • What are the best academic papers or research studies about [topic]?

Content Comprehensiveness

Explain [topic] in detail, citing the most comprehensive and reliable sources.

Tests if your content is considered a complete resource.

Variations

  • What are all the important aspects of [topic] that should be considered?
  • Create a complete guide about [topic], using authoritative sources.
  • What are the advanced concepts related to [topic] that experts discuss?

Definition Accuracy

What is the most accurate and complete definition of [term/concept]? Cite your sources.

Tests if your content's definitions are referenced.

Variations

  • How do experts define [term/concept]?
  • What are the key components of [term/concept] according to authoritative sources?
  • Compare different definitions of [term/concept] from reliable sources.

Prompt Usage Best Practices

Testing Frequency

  • Run basic tests weekly
  • Perform comprehensive tests monthly
  • Test after significant content updates
  • Monitor citation patterns continuously

Result Analysis

  • Document citation patterns
  • Track changes over time
  • Compare across content types
  • Identify improvement opportunities
  • Adjust optimization strategy

Optimization Workflow

  • Start with basic authority tests
  • Progress to comprehensive analysis
  • Focus on weak areas
  • Implement improvements systematically
  • Retest after changes
