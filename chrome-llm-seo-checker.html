Privacy Policy for LLM SEO Checker

Last Updated: May 2024

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how the LLM SEO Checker Chrome extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles your data.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: This permission is required to analyze the content of the currently active webpage. We only access the tab when you explicitly click the extension icon or use its features. This permission does not allow access to your browsing history or other tabs.

Data Collection and Usage

The LLM SEO Checker extension operates entirely within your browser and analyzes only the currently active webpage. We collect and process the following information:

  • Content of the webpage you are currently viewing (for analysis purposes only)
  • Presence of specific files (robots.txt, sitemap.xml, llms.txt) on the website being analyzed

Data Storage and Transmission

The extension:

  • Does NOT store any data permanently
  • Does NOT transmit any data to external servers
  • Does NOT collect any personal information
  • Processes all data locally within your browser
  • Automatically clears all temporary data when you close the analysis window

Third-Party Services

The extension includes a link to llmlogs.com for additional services. When you click this link, you will be subject to the privacy policy of that website.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. The extension's analysis results are temporary and exist only during your active use of the extension.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the extension, please visit https://llmlogs.com/contact.
