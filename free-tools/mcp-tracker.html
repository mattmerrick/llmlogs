About MCP Score

What is MCP?

Mean Cumulative Precision (MCP) measures how well your content performs in LLM responses, considering both citation frequency and position.

How It's Calculated

MCP takes into account the position of citations in LLM responses, giving higher weight to earlier mentions.

Improving Your Score

Focus on content quality, proper citations, and clear structure to improve your MCP score.
