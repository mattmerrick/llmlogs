About This Tool

This Knowledge Graph Validator helps you ensure your structured data is properly formatted for LLM knowledge graphs. It validates:

  • JSON-LD syntax and structure
  • Schema.org compliance
  • Entity relationships and connections
  • Property completeness and accuracy
  • Semantic consistency and coherence
