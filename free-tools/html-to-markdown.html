Why Convert HTML to Markdown?

Markdown is a lightweight markup language that's perfect for LLM optimization. Converting your HTML to Markdown can help:

  • Improve content readability for AI models
  • Reduce unnecessary formatting overhead
  • Make your content more accessible to LLMs
  • Simplify content structure while maintaining formatting
